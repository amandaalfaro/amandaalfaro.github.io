What is GHC?



  • Date: Tuesday, October 18th- Friday, October 21st

    Location: George Convention Center Houston, Texas

    Registration Opens: July 13th at 9 am PT


    Every year, GHC brings together the community of women technologists, the best minds in computing and increased visibility for the contributions of women to computing. The conference is co-presented by the Association of Computing Machinery (ACM).

    The conference results in collaborative proposals, networking and mentoring for our attendees. Conference presenters are leaders in their respective fields, representing industry, academia and government. The conference also offers professional development through a variety of activities.

Why Attend the Grace Hopper Conference?



  • If you are a computer science major you can attend! If you care about empowering women in tech, you can attend! No GPA requirements and no experience needed.

    Attending the Grace Hopper Conference can lead to full-time and internship opportunities, networking with other students from around the world, and learn new skills to help you throughout your professional career.
