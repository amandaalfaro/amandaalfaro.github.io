What is included in the fee?


Free food at ALL general meetings and events! Not only pizza, we love ordering Jimmy John's!

Free help on harder classes with coding projects and higher math courses. So no need to dish out $$$ for a tutor when you can ask someone in WICS who has taken the same class!

Free registration for Game Jam (specs listed below)

What is included in the game jam?


Game Jam is free to all club members! Includes free breakfast and lunch on Saturday and Sunday AND free dinner on Friday and Saturday

Free swag from companies like: portfolio for career fairs or work, buttons to style your backpacks and stickers!

If your game happens to win any of the chances winners will receive a tablet, games provided by EA and t-shirts!

All participants receive free WICS t-shirts
