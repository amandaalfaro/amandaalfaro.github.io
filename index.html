About Us


Women in Computer Science at LSU was founded by 7 LSU computer science students during the 2015 Spring semester. Our mission is to provide an academic, social, and professional community for women studying computer science at LSU. We hold regular tutoring sessions, professional workshops, socials, and other events, all of which are open to LSU students.
